Parameter-free category-conditioned routing: repurposes dropout to drive modular specialization with no learned gating network. Stability-guided freezing locks converged modules to cut training compute.

My contribution: the specialization metrics (module–category mutual information, pruning sensitivity) and the per-domain evaluation; validated on CIFAR-100 ResNets and 60–125M-parameter MoE LMs.
