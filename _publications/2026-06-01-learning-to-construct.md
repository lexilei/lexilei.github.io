A framework that casts agent systems as code, enabling automatic construction of efficient agent systems.

The companion cross-benchmark study maps when primitive induction helps across 19 reasoning tasks: gains scale with baseline-tool weakness — e.g. +8.2pp on MedCalc at ~60% fewer input tokens, a quality–efficiency Pareto gain.
